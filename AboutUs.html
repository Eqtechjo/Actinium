About Us

About Actinium

Actinium is a leading provider of high-quality laboratory and scientific supplies based in Jordan. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to supporting the scientific, pharmaceutical, educational, healthcare, and industrial sectors with advanced solutions that meet both local and international standards.

Mission Statement

To empower scientific excellence and technological innovation across Jordan and the MENA region by delivering advanced laboratory solutions, precision instruments, and technical expertise. We are committed to enabling breakthroughs in research, healthcare, and industry by aligning global standards with local needs, ensuring reliability, compliance, and long-term value for our partners.

Vision Statement

To be the leading catalyst for scientific and analytical progress in Jordan and the wider region, recognized for our integrity, innovation, and impact. We envision a future where every laboratory — academic, clinical, or industrial — is equipped to operate at the highest international standards.

Our Expertise

We specialize in supplying analytical and precision instruments, laboratory consumables and chemicals, microbiological and pharmaceutical testing materials, environmental and water testing equipment, educational lab solutions, and calibration & validation support.

Global Partnerships

We proudly collaborate with globally recognized manufacturers and brands to ensure our clients receive reliable, innovative, and compliant tools that enhance their scientific capabilities.
